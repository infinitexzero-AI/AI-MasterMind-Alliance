# Sovereign OS: Master Financial & Tax Index (NB, Canada)

A lifelong organizational roadmap for personal, student, and business bookkeeping, structured to satisfy the Canada Revenue Agency (CRA) compliance standards.

---

## 📂 Lifelong Directory Taxonomy

### 1. 💼 Business Records (Sole Proprietorship / T2125)
*Standardized framework for tracking self-employment revenue and expenses.*

- **Target Path:** `/02_Resources/Finances/Tax_Vault/[Year]/03_Business_T2125`
- **Key Sub-folders / Sections:**
  - `Revenue_and_Invoices` (All incoming payments, contracts)
  - `Operating_Expenses` (T2125 Parts 4 & 5: Office, Internet, Software)
  - `Motor_Vehicle_Log` (Part 17: Mileage, Fuel, Insurance splits)
  - `Home_Office_Usage` (Part 7: Square footage, Utilities, Rent)
  - `GST_HST_Returns` (New Brunswick 15% HST filing records)

### 2. 🎓 Student & Academic Records (T2202 / Scholarships)
*Optimization checklist for tuition credits and student-related deductions.*

- **Target Path:** `/02_Resources/Finances/Tax_Vault/[Year]/02_Student_Records`
- **Key Sub-folders / Sections:**
  - `Tuition_Slips_T2202` (Official university/college tax certificates)
  - `Scholarships_Bursaries_T4A` (Taxable and non-taxable grants)
  - `Student_Loan_Interest` (Deductible interest payment receipts)

### 3. 👤 Personal Income & Tax Credits
*Core repository for standard individual income streams and provincial credits.*

- **Target Path:** `/02_Resources/Finances/Tax_Vault/[Year]/01_Personal_Income_and_Credits`
- **Key Sub-folders / Sections:**
  - `Employment_Income_T4` (Standard wage slips)
  - `Medical_Receipts` (Provincial threshold tracking)
  - `Charitable_Donations` (Registered charity slips)
