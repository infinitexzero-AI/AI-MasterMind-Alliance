# ADMIN TRIAGE PROTOCOL: Vanguard Standard (2026)

**Objective:** Transform scattered inputs (Email, Moodle, Teams) into a prioritized execution list in < 15 minutes.

---

## 🌅 Phase 1: The Capture Pulse (08:30 - 08:45)

1. **Open Outlook/MTA Email**: Copy the subject lines of all unread messages from the last 24 hours.
2. **Open Moodle**: Copy the titles and due dates of any upcoming assignments/forums.
3. **Open Teams**: Copy any direct requests or project updates.
4. **Action**: Paste all raw text into the **Vanguard AI Command Matrix -> Capture Portal**.

---

## ⚖️ Phase 2: The Priority Filter

Use the **Eisenhower Decision Logic** provided by the AI Command Matrix:

- **Quadrant 1 (Do Now)**: Legal deadlines, high-weight exams, bank freeze rebuttals.
- **Quadrant 2 (Schedule)**: Tax restructuring, GPA recovery planning, deep-work study blocks.
- **Quadrant 3 (Delegate/Batch)**: Standard administrative emails, meeting requests.
- **Quadrant 4 (Eliminate)**: Non-essential research, noise.

---

## 🏹 Phase 3: The Prompt Execution

Use the **Prompt Forge** in your Matrix to handle specific domains:

- **[Morning Triage]**: Paste your captured text to get the urgency-ranked list.
- **[Moodle Digest]**: Specifically for extracting study objectives and deadlines.
- **[Legal/CRA Pulse]**: For checking the status of ongoing rebuttals.

---

## 📅 Phase 4: The Sync

Update your local `task.md` or the HTML Matrix Dashboard with the results of your triage.

> [!TIP]
> **Compound Knowledge:** As you complete tasks in each domain, document the "Key Learning" in the Master Knowledge Base. This ensures your legal literacy and academic performance compound over time.
