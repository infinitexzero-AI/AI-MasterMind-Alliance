Course Description/Objectives:
This course introduces the students to Canada’s laws and legal systems, with an emphasis on the law regulating commercial activity in Canada. 

The focus is on an introduction to Canadian commercial law and related legal issues, principles and legal relationships impacting commercial/business activity.

Supported by a textbook and supplemental readings, students will be guided through discussion on various legal topics and the resulting implications on business, managers and decision-makers. 

REQUIRED TEXT: Managing the law: The legal Aspects of Doing Business. 6th Edition (Pearson) (Authors: McInnes/Kerr/Vanduzer)
