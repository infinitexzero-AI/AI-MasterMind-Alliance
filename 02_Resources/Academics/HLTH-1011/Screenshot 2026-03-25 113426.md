# INCENTIVES & COERCION
The desire to reward or thank participants must be balanced with the need for participation in any research project to be voluntary.
- *Voluntariness* is a decision made of an individual's own free will without undue outside influence.
- *Coercion* involves compelling an individual to participate in a research study in violation of the principles of autonomy and respect for persons.
Researchers must be transparent about what participants will gain from participation in a research study and what they will not gain.
