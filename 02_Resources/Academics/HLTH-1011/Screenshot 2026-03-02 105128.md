# Ecosystem Services
- 1,425 million hectares of remaining wetlands (across 11 types) provide an estimated $7.98 trillion (median 2023 Int$) to $39.01 trillion (mean 2023 Int$) in annual benefits to people.
- If effectively managed until 2050, these wetlands are projected to provide a net present value (NPV) greater than $205.25 trillion (median 2023 Int$) over this time period.
