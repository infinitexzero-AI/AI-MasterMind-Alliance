# Global production of staple crops, with projections for 2025
- This slide presents bar charts illustrating the global production of six staple crops (Corn, Soybean, Rice, Wheat, Sorghum, Millet), measured in millions of tonnes, from approximately 2000 to 2025/26.
- Data for 2025/26 is preliminary, based on the September 2025 release from the US Department of Agriculture.
- **Corn, Soybean, Rice, and Wheat** show clear upward trends in global production over the period, indicating increasing output.
- **Sorghum and Millet** production appear relatively stable or fluctuating year-to-year, without a significant long-term increase.
- The source for all data is the US Department of Agriculture, World Agricultural Production (September 2025 Revision).
