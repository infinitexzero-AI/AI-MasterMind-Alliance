# Forest cover INDIA
- India's total forest and tree cover is 827,357 sq km, accounting for 25.17% of the country's total geographical area.
- This total comprises 715,343 sq km (21.76%) of forest cover and 112,014 sq km (3.41%) of tree cover.
- A map of India visually represents the distribution of forest cover, with a legend indicating the percentage of area covered from 0% (yellow) to 100% (dark green).
- The map data is sourced from Copernicus Global Land Service: Land Cover 100m and copyrighted by Milos Popovic (2024).
