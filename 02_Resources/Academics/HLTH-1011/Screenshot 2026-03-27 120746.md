# Research Questions and Research Aim
## Research Questions:
- *Explore the relationship between improved outcomes and cultural safety, case management and community development by developing an understanding of how service providers adapt case management and programming to "make them work" in ways that are culturally safe for the people they serve.*
- *Does linking case management and community development programs and services improve health and social outcomes for people living with HIV, HCV, ill mental health, and/or problematic substance use, with a focus on exploring outcomes for Indigenous populations?*
- *Do Indigenous service delivery models, based on an Indigenous worldview of health and wellbeing with explicit focus on cultural safety, produce improved health and social outcomes for people living with HIV, HCV, ill mental health, and/or problematic substance use (regardless of Indigenous ancestry)?*
## Research Aim
- ***Why, when, how, and for whom, does community-based services work well***
