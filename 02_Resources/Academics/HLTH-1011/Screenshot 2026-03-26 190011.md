# Hydrological Cycle in an Agricultural Context
- **Rainfall (R=100%)**: Water falling from a cloud onto the ground.
- **Transpiration (T=15-30%)**: Water released by plants (e.g., corn) into the atmosphere.
- **Evaporation (E=30-50%)**: Water evaporating from the soil surface into the atmosphere.
- **Surface runoff (Roff=10-25%)**: Water flowing over the surface of the land.
- **Soil infiltration (S)**: Water penetrating into the soil below the surface.
- **Deep percolation (D=10-30%)**: Water moving into deeper soil layers.
