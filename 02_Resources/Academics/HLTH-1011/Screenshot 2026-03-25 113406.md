# Sample Ethical Considerations for Research Projects
- This table outlines ethical considerations for both individual and community-based research, categorized by four core principles.
- **Respect:** Addresses protecting individual rights, ensuring informed consent, privacy, confidentiality, and respecting community values and consultation.
- **Beneficence:** Questions how individuals and the participating community will benefit from the research.
- **Nonmaleficence:** Focuses on minimizing physical, psychological, financial, social, and other risks to individual participants, and ensuring the community is not burdened.
- **Justice:** Examines the long-term benefits for individual participants (e.g., increased knowledge, result sharing) and the community (e.g., ongoing relationship).
