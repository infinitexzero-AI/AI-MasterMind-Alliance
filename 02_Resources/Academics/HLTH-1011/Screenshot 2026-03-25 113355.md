# ETHICS PRINCIPLES
- **Respect for persons** emphasizes autonomy, informed consent, voluntariness, and protection of potentially vulnerable individuals
  - *Autonomy*: only an individual (or his or her legal guardian) is authorized to decide whether to volunteer to participate in a research study
- **Beneficence** means that the study should do good by maximizing possible benefits and minimizing possible harms
  - *Non-maleficence* means that the study should do no harm
- **Distributive justice** requires the benefits and burdens of research to be fairly allocated
