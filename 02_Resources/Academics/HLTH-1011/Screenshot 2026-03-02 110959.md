# Nova Scotia comparison
- The Nova Scotia Wetland Conservation Policy was released on October 14, 2011, providing context to existing legislation, regulations, and operational strategies for protecting and managing wetlands (e.g., Environment Act, Environmental Goals and Sustainable Prosperity Act).
- The policy's goal is to prevent the **net loss** of wetland in Nova Scotia by integrating wetland protection with the need for sustainable economic development.
- One objective is to manage human activity near wetlands, aiming for **no loss** in Wetlands of Special Significance and preventing **net loss** in area and function for other wetlands, aligning with No Net Loss (NNL) Policies.
