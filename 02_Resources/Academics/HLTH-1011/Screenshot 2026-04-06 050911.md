# Freshwater Systems
## Goods
- Drinking water
- Irrigation water
- Fish
- Hydroelectricity
- genetic resources
## Services
- Buffer water flow (control timing and volume)
- Dilute and carry away wastes
- Cycle nutrients
- Sequester carbon
- Maintain biodiversity
- Provide aquatic habitat
- Provide transportation corridor
- Provide employment
- Provide for aesthetic enjoyment and recreation
