# Global SDG6 Index: Clean Water and Sanitation
- A world choropleth map displays the SDG6 Index, representing global progress on clean water and sanitation.
- A color legend indicates index scores from 0 (red, lowest performance) to 100 (dark blue, highest performance), with gray for 'No data'.
- Countries in North America, Western Europe, Australia, and parts of South America and East Asia predominantly show high SDG6 Index scores (blue/green).
- Many countries across Africa, the Middle East, and South Asia exhibit lower SDG6 Index scores (yellow/orange/red), indicating significant challenges in achieving clean water and sanitation goals.
- The map also includes a compass rose and a scale bar in kilometers.
