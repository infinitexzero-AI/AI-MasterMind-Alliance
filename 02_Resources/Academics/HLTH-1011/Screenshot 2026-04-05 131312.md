# Key Principles of Green Building Design
- **Energy Efficiency & Renewable Energy:** Passive design utilizes natural sunlight for heating and daylighting, reducing reliance on electric lighting by 30-50%. High-performance envelopes and insulation are key.
- **Renewable Energy Integration:** Technologies like solar panels (photovoltaics) reduce dependence on the grid.
- **Water Efficiency:** Techniques include low-flow fixtures, rainwater harvesting, and greywater recycling for irrigation.
- **Sustainable Materials:** Using recycled, non-toxic, and local materials reduces the carbon footprint and lowers VOC emissions for better air quality.
- **Passive Cooling & Ventilation:** Techniques such as wind towers, operable windows, and shading devices (louvers) minimize air conditioning needs.
- **Biophilic Design:** Integrating natural light, plants, and natural materials improves occupant health and well-being.
