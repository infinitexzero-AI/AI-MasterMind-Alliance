# Wetlands and Ecosystem Services
-   **Ecosystem Services Definition:** Benefits humans derive from natural ecosystems, vital for human health, economic development, and quality of life.
-   **Global Economic Contribution:** Provide 40.6% of total global ecosystem services, valued at ~US$47.4 trillion per year.
-   **Canadian Wetlands Value:** Estimated to provide $225 billion/year in ecosystem services, ~10% of GDP.
-   **Ontario Wetlands Valuation Examples:**
    -   Suspended sediment and phosphorus filtration/removal: $4.2 billion/year (versus cost of $2.9 billion/year for artificial replacement).
    -   Filtration ability: $40 billion in annual economic benefits.
    -   Greenbelt wetlands ecological services: $1.3 billion.
-   **Key Ecosystem Services Illustrated (Infographic by MNRF, 2015):**
    -   Water Quality Improvement
    -   Wildlife Habitat
    -   Fish Habitat
    -   Flood Mitigation
    -   Erosion Reduction
    -   Cultural and Spiritual Significance
    -   Climate Change Mitigation
    -   Groundwater Recharge and Discharge
    -   Recreation and Tourism
    -   Source of Food and Medicines
