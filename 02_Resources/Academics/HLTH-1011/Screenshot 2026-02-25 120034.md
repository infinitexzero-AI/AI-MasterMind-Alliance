# Misclassification Bias
- Mislabeling or mischaracterizing a study subject, may occur with disease or exposure.
- Similarly, one can misclassify exposure. For example, people may underestimate behaviours that are socially undesirable (e.g., # cigarettes or # drinks per day)
- Misclassification can result in under- or overestimating the effect of a certain behaviour on a health outcome
