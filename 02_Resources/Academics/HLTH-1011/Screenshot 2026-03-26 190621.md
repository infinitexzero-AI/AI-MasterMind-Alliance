# Table 3.1 Water productivity and crop water requirements
- This table presents data on water productivity (m³ of green water flow per tonne grain) and crop water requirements (mm) for major food crops worldwide.
- Information is organized into columns for Crop, Hydroclimate Type, Type of Crop (C3 or C4), Water productivity Range and Average, Source, Yield range (t/ha), and Water requirements (mm).
- The crops are broadly divided into temperate (e.g., Wheat, Barley, Oats, Rapeseed, Green Beans, Green Peas, Potatoes) and tropical categories (e.g., Rice, Maize, Millet, Sorghum, Sugarcane, Cotton seed, Sunflower seed, Dry Beans, Soya beans, Banana/plantain, Oranges).
- Specific numerical values for water productivity, yield, and water requirements are provided for each crop, along with the scientific sources for the data.
- A note references Doorenbos and Kassam 1986; Doorenbos and Pruitt 1992 for further details.
