# Experience of a trial participant

This flowchart illustrates the steps a participant undertakes in a clinical trial:
-   **Initial Contact:** A participant enters a Family Planning Clinic and provides informed consent for screening.
-   **Screening Visit 1:** This visit includes education about the trial, HIV and pregnancy testing, sexually transmitted infection (STI) tests and treatment, and the collection of baseline data. Following this, informed consent to enroll in the main trial is obtained.
-   **Screening Visit 2:** Participants receive their test results, undergo counselling, and receive reinforced education about the trial.
-   **Randomisation:** The participant is then randomly assigned by chance to one of two groups:
    -   A group receiving Condoms + experimental gel
    -   A group receiving Condoms + placebo
