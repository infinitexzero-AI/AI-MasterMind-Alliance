# Experience of a trial participant
-   **Family Planning Clinic Entry:** Participant begins at a Family Planning Clinic.
-   **Informed Consent for Screening:** Participant gives informed consent for initial screening.
-   **Screening Visit 1:** This visit involves education about the trial, HIV and pregnancy tests, sexually transmitted infection tests and treatment, and collection of baseline data.
-   **Informed Consent to Enrol:** Participant provides informed consent to formally enrol in the trial.
-   **Screening Visit 2:** During this visit, participants receive test results, counselling, and reinforced education about the trial.
-   **Randomisation:** Participants are then randomly assigned by chance to one of two trial groups.
-   **Trial Groups:**
    -   Condoms + experimental gel
    -   Condoms + placebo
