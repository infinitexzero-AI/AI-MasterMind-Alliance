# Experience of a trial participant
This slide illustrates the journey of a participant in a clinical trial:
-   **Entry:** A participant visits a Family Planning Clinic.
-   **Screening Consent:** The participant provides informed consent for initial screening.
-   **Screening Visit 1:** Involves education on the trial, HIV, pregnancy, and sexually transmitted infection testing/treatment, and baseline data collection.
-   **Enrolment Consent:** The participant provides informed consent to formally enrol in the trial.
-   **Screening Visit 2:** Includes reviewing test results, counselling, and reinforcing education about the trial.
-   **Randomisation:** The participant is then randomly assigned by chance to one of two groups:
    -   **Group 1:** Receives Condoms + experimental gel.
    -   **Group 2:** Receives Condoms + placebo.
