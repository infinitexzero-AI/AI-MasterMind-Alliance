# How did this project use a two-eyed seeing approach?
- Recruited an Indigenous Principal Investigator (researcher) at the beginning of the project.
- We engaged Elders from the beginning and throughout our project who guided and reviewed the work we were doing.
- Community organizations were Indigenous or mainly served Indigenous people.
- Indigenous people were hired as research assistants, in particular Indigenous people with lived and/or living experience with STBBI'S, mental health and substance use.
- The perspectives and voices of Indigenous people were prioritized in the analysis.
