# Hear Us, See Us, Respect Us

## Respecting the Expertise of People Who Use Drugs

# Fair Compensation for PWUD Participating in Conferences, Meetings and Other Events

- Event conveners should pay People Who Use Drugs (PWUD) for their contributions and participation, recognizing their unique expertise.
- Standard practice dictates covering travel and accommodation, and providing per diems at the national rate.
- PWUD should be paid in cash, at a daily rate comparable to the host organization's own employees.
- An additional honorarium should be provided if PWUD are invited to speak or present.
- Details such as hotel deposits (e.g., credit card), medications, and childcare needs must be clarified before departure.
- PWUD should be given the opportunity to state their preference regarding the form and timing of payment.
