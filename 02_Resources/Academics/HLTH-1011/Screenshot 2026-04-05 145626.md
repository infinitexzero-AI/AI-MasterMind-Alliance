# Solutions for Sustainability: We have choices!
- Spatial scale
    - Avoid fragmentation (of land and authority)
    - Encourage walkability
- Parks and pathways
    - Green design and sponge city principles for health and fitness
- Air quality
    - Contain sprawl; encourage public transit
- waste management
    - Regulatory environment in support of reducing waste streams
- Transportation
    - Incentives and opportunities to use transit, bicycles
