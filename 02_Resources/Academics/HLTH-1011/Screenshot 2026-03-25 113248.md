# GUIDANCE IN RESEARCH ETHICS
- Nuremberg Code (1947): mandated voluntary consent for experimental studies of humans.
- Declaration of Helsinki (1964): written by the World Medical Association to provide guidelines for physicians conducting clinical trials.
- Tri-Council Policy Statement: Ethical Conduct for Research Involving Humans (2022): joint policy of Canada's three federal research agencies outlining commitment to the people of Canada to promote the ethical conduct of research involving humans.
- Mount Allison University Research Ethics Board: approve, reject, propose modifications to, or terminate any proposed or ongoing research involving human subjects which is conducted within or by members of MTA using the considerations set forth in the TCPS2 as the minimum standard.
