# Impact Evaluation
- “impact” = a positive or negative, primary or secondary long-term effect produced by an intervention, directly or indirectly, and intended or unintended
    - Ultimate changes that you want to occur in individuals, organizations, communities or systems as a result of program activities
- assesses program effectiveness in achieving its ultimate goals
- has to not only provide credible evidence that changes have occurred but also undertake credible causal inference that these changes have been at least partly due to a project, program or policy.
