# Worldwide pesticide Use
- This line graph illustrates the global trend of pesticide use in tonnes from 1990 to 2016.
- The Y-axis shows the quantity in Tonnes, ranging from 2,000,000 to 4,500,000.
- The X-axis represents the Year, from 1990 to 2016.
- The graph indicates a significant overall increase in worldwide pesticide use, starting around 2.2 million tonnes in 1990 and rising to over 4 million tonnes by 2016, with minor fluctuations throughout the period.
