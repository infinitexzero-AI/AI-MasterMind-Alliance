# Climate Change Vulnerability Index 2014
- Presents a world map illustrating countries' vulnerability to climate change using a color gradient from green (Low Risk) to red (Extreme Risk).
- Regions in Africa and South/Southeast Asia are depicted with the highest levels of vulnerability.
- Key countries highlighted on the map include Haiti, Guinea-Bissau, Sierra Leone, Nigeria, DR Congo, Ethiopia, South Sudan, Bangladesh, Philippines, and Cambodia.
- A table lists the top 10 countries with 'Extreme' vulnerability:
    - 1. Bangladesh
    - 2. Guinea-Bissau
    - 3. Sierra Leone
    - 4. Haiti
    - 5. South Sudan
    - 6. Nigeria
    - 7. DR Congo
    - 8. Cambodia
    - 9. Philippines
    - 10. Ethiopia
- The data source is Maplecroft, 2013.
