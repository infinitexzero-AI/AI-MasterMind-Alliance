# Public Transit Usage Trends (2023-2025)
- **Commuter Usage (May 2024):** 11.4% of employed Canadians (1.6 million) used public transit as their main commute, an increase from 7.8% in May 2021.
- **Pre-pandemic Comparison:** The May 2024 usage rate (11.4%) remained below the 12.6% recorded in May 2016.
- **Ridership Levels (2025):** Urban transit ridership in 2025 stalled, reaching 82% to 86% of 2019 pre-pandemic levels.
- **Usage by Demographic (2024):** Hybrid workers (25.5%) were more than twice as likely to use public transit compared to regular commuters (11.4%) in May 2024.
