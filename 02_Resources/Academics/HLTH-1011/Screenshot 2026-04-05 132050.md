# Key Urban Environmental Management Issues
- Air pollution
    - Ground level ozone; particulate matter
    - Temperature inversion in winter; geography (Denver, Mexico City)
- Heat island effect
    - Green roof technology; north-south road orientation; light colored surfaces; increased urban vegetation
- Hydrological cycle
    - Flooding and runoff; aquifer depletion; pollution
    - 'sponge city' design
- Brownfield sites
    - Abandoned or active industrial sites; surface/underground soil contamination
- Vulnerability to natural hazards
    - Hurricanes, earthquakes, tsunami; “build back better”
