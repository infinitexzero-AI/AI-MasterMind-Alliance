# Wetlands Policy Context and Framework

This slide displays a comprehensive overview of the policy and framework for wetlands at various jurisdictional levels. On the left, there is a photograph of an individual standing on a hill, looking out over a vast wetland landscape under a cloudy sky. The right side outlines the key policies and initiatives:

## International Level
* Ramsar Convention
* The North American Waterfowl Management Plan
* Regional/transboundary plans and policies
    * Great Lakes Wetland Action Plan
    * Plan 2000 for the St Lawrence River System
    * Fraser River Action Plan

## Federal level
* Federal policy on wetland conservation (1991) directly applied to federal lands (29% of Canadian wetlands)
* Environmental Protection Act
* Wide variety of Federal statutes: e.g. Water Act, Migratory Birds Convention Act, Canada Wildlife Act, National Parks Act, Canada Oceans Act, Fisheries Act, Canada Environmental Assessment Act, Income Tax Act of Canada
