# Hierarchy of Evidence

This pyramid illustrates the quality and risk of bias for different types of research studies, progressing from lowest quality/highest bias at the base to highest quality/lowest bias at the apex:
- **Bottom Level:** Expert Opinion / Background Information (Promising, Unfiltered Information)
- **Mid Levels (Primary Studies):** Case Report, Case Control Study, Cohort Study, Randomized Controlled Trial (RCT) (Supported, Unfiltered Information)
- **Top Levels (Secondary Studies):** Meta Analysis, Systematic Review (Well-Supported, Filtered Information)

The diagram also indicates that "Risk of bias" decreases from bottom to top, while "Quality" increases, aligning with the progression from unfiltered to filtered information.
