# Hierarchy of Evidence

This pyramid illustrates the ranking of different types of evidence, from highest quality and support to lowest, and increasing risk of bias from top to bottom.

- **Systematic Review:** (Well-Supported, Secondary Study, Filtered Information)
- **Meta Analysis:** (Well-Supported, Secondary Study, Filtered Information)
- **Randomized Controlled Trial (RCT):** (Supported, Primary Study, Unfiltered Information)
- **Cohort Study:** (Supported, Primary Study, Unfiltered Information)
- **Case Control Study:** (Supported, Primary Study, Unfiltered Information)
- **Case Report:** (Supported, Primary Study, Unfiltered Information)
- **Expert Opinion / Background Information:** (Promising, Primary Study, Unfiltered Information)

**Overall Trends:** Risk of Bias increases downwards, while Quality and level of Support decrease downwards.
