# Build Up, Not Out

The image illustrates the multiple positive effects of population density:
- **More Homes:** Reduces housing shortages in the city.
- **More People:** Increases population in the neighbourhood.
- **Increased Services:** Locally, due to a larger customer base.
- **Walking- and Cycling Distance:** Simplifies daily life with closer access to functions.
- **Public Transport:** Fewer cars needed, more frequent public transport use.
- **Greater Security:** More movement in areas during daytime and evenings.
- **Increased Variety:** Of culture, shops, and public services closer by.
- **Enhanced City Life:** More vibrant city life and urban environments.
- **Better Parks and Playgrounds:** Improved recreational spaces.
- **Attractive Living:** Desirable and reasonably priced areas.
- **Higher Land Values:** Enabling further construction.
