# Socio-political

This slide presents a table outlining key socio-political aspects and corresponding questions for analysis.

| Aspect                        | Question                                                                                                    |
| :---------------------------- | :---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Land rights/tenure            | Do people have secure land rights? On the basis of what type of legal system?                               |
| Water rights                  | Do people have secure water rights? On the basis of what type of legal system?                              |
| Land management practices     | Have practices adapted to changed contexts?                                                                 |
| Innovation                    | How have farmers innovated through time and at what scale? Are these innovations fungible/transferable?       |
| Access to or support for innovation | How are farmer-initiated innovations supported? How are innovations disseminated into/across farming communities? |
| Access to resources/support   | Are there government-supported extension services? Are there NGOs or CSOs on the ground?                    |
| Political interest/will       | Is the government (local/national) an active and supportive player, an obstruction, or a threat?           |
