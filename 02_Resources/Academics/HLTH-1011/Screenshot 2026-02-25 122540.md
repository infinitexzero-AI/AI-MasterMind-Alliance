# The product pipeline
- This diagram illustrates the sequential stages of a product development pipeline, often seen in pharmaceutical or medical device development.
- It starts with 'Early-stage concepts', represented by numerous dotted circles.
- Concepts then move into 'Preclinical testing', shown as a reduced number of light blue circles.
- The next stage is 'Early human safety trials', represented by an even smaller cluster of dark blue circles.
- The final stage depicted is 'Large-scale efficacy trials', symbolized by a single, larger dark blue circle.
- Arrows indicate progression between stages, visually demonstrating a funneling process where the number of concepts narrows as they advance through testing.
