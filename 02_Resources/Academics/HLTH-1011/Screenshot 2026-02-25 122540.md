# The product pipeline
- This diagram illustrates the product development pipeline, showing a progressive reduction from a wide range of initial ideas to a select few products entering advanced trials.
- The stages depicted are:
    - Early-stage concepts (represented by numerous dashed circles)
    - Preclinical testing (fewer solid light blue circles)
    - Early human safety trials (a smaller group of brighter blue circles)
    - Large-scale efficacy trials (a single dark blue circle, representing the final stage for a successful product)
