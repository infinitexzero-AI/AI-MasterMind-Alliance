# Criteria for Assessing Validity of Quantitative Studies
- **Reliability**: The data collection instruments used provided accurate, consistent measures of the attributes or variables the researcher intended to measure.
- **Internal validity**: The research design adequately controlled for extraneous variables and eliminated plausible rival explanations for the research findings.
- **External validity**: The research employed a sampling strategy that permits the generalization of the results beyond the specific research participants, research setting and time period.
