# Making it work Research Project

- This is an Indigenous-focused, community-based research project in British Columbia, co-led by PAN and the AHA Centre at CAAN (Communities, Alliances and Networks).
- The project investigated integrated community-based services for individuals living with HIV, hepatitis C, or challenges related to mental health or substance use.
- The study focused on:
    - Services delivered through an Indigenous worldview and how organizations establish cultural safety for their clients.
    - Gaining insight into whether individuals facing these challenges achieve better outcomes when accessing services that align with an Indigenous view of health and wellbeing.
    - Linking case management services with community development programs.
