# Key Challenges
1.  **Quality vs quantity problem**
    *   Not all "protected areas" meet ecological integrity standards
    *   Risk of "paper parks" or weak protections
2.  **Southern Canada gap**
    *   Most biodiversity (about 70%) is in southern, heavily developed regions
    *   Harder (and politically costly) to protect land there (e.g. due to private property rights)
3.  **Indigenous governance**
    *   Canada's success increasingly depends on (i) Recognition of Indigenous land rights; and (ii) Co-governance and Indigenous-led conservation
    *   Indigenous Protected and Conserved Areas (IPCAs) are central to closing the gap
4.  **Connectivity and representativeness**
    *   CBD requires: (i) Ecologically representative systems; (ii) Connected landscapes
    *   Canada still over-represents remote boreal and Arctic regions; and under-represents grasslands, wetlands, and southern ecosystems
