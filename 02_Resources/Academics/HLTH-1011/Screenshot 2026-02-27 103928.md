# UN Water Integrated Monitoring Initiative for SDG 6

This slide illustrates the monitoring framework for Sustainable Development Goal 6 (SDG 6): Clean Water and Sanitation. It features a circular diagram detailing eleven target areas, each with a specific indicator code, icon, and description.

## SDG 6 Target Areas (Highlighted sections in pink are 6.5.1 and 6.5.2):
- **6.1.1** Drinking Water
- **6.2.1a** Sanitation
- **6.2.1b** Hygiene
- **6.3.1** Wastewater
- **6.3.2** Water Quality
- **6.4.1** Water-Use Efficiency
- **6.4.2** Water Stress
- **6.5.1** Integrated Water Resources Management
- **6.5.2** Transboundary Cooperation
- **6.6.1** Ecosystems
- **6.a.1** International Cooperation
- **6.b.1** Participation

Around the diagram, various monitoring programs and organizations are listed as contributors (e.g., UN-WATER GLAAS, WHO/UNICEF JMP, FAO AQUASTAT).

| **6.3.2** Proportion of bodies of water with good ambient water quality | UNEP |
| **6.4.1** Change in water-use efficiency over time | FAO |
