# Ecosystem Services
- The 1,425 million hectares of remaining wetlands (across 11 types) provide an estimated annual benefit to people ranging from $7.98 trillion (median 2023 Int$) to $39.01 trillion (mean 2023 Int$).
- If effectively managed until 2050, these wetlands will yield a net present value (NPV) greater than $205.25 trillion (median 2023 Int$) over this period.
