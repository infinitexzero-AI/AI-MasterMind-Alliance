# International Rivers
- 286 international river basins
- Cover 42% of earth's surface
- 151 countries of which ½ have 80% of territory 2/3 have 50% of territory in IRB
- 2.8 billion people (42% of world population)
- 145 international treaties since 1814 established to deal with some non-navigational use aspect
- > half of rivers have no formal cooperative arrangements
- 1997 culminate in UN Convention on Non-Navigational Uses of Internationally Shared Watercourses (entered into law, 17 August 2014)
