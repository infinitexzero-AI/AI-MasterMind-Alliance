# International Rivers
- 286 international river basins, covering 42% of the Earth's surface.
- 151 countries; half of which have 80% of their territory in International River Basins (IRB), and two-thirds have 50% of their territory in IRBs.
- 2.8 billion people (42% of the world population) live in IRBs.
- 145 international treaties since 1814 have been established to deal with non-navigational use aspects.
- Over half of rivers have no formal cooperative arrangements.
- The UN Convention on Non-Navigational Uses of Internationally Shared Watercourses, which culminated in 1997, entered into law on August 17, 2014.
