# Walvis Bay, Namibia
- Population grew from approximately 23,000 in 1991 to over 102,000 by 2023, with an estimated 103,000 by 2025.
- The town serves as Namibia's primary port, experiencing rapid urbanization and a 347% population increase since independence.
- Aerial image shows Walvis Bay, a coastal town with port facilities, a bay with boats, and arid surroundings.
