# Doctrines of Water Allocation (Cont'd)

- **Limited Territorial Sovereignty/Integrity:** A state can utilize waters from a shared river as long as its use does not impede a co-riparian's ability to also use the water.
- **Community of interests:** State boundaries should be disregarded, with the drainage basin considered the primary economic and physical unit. Interventions within the basin must be planned in consultation with all basin members.
