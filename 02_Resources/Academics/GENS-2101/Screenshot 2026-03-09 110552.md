# Factors accounting for consistent decline in total harvest
- Collapse of the U.S. Housing Market (Most Immediate Cause)
- Structural Decline in Some Forest Product Markets (e.g. newsprint)
- Mountain Pine Beetle and Changing Timber Supply (Western Canada)
- Environmental Policy and Conservation Measures (old growth protection)
- Trade Disputes and Market Access Issues (soft-wood lumber)
- Mill Closures and Industry Consolidation
- Fibre Supply Constraints and Disturbances
- Shift of Investment to the United States
