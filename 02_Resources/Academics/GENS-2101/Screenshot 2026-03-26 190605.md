# Table 7.2 Management Strategies to Improve Green Water Productivity

-   **Vapour Shift Strategies:** Focus on reducing evaporation through methods like dry planting, mulching, zero tillage, intercropping, windbreaks, and agroforestry. These aim for quick crop establishment, reduced soil exposure, and increased canopy cover. 
-   **Improve T/ET Ratio Strategies:** Aim to increase plant water uptake and productive green water flow. This involves using improved crop varieties, water harvesting, soil and water conservation, soil fertility management, conservation tillage, and intercropping to mitigate dry spells, maximize infiltration, water holding capacity (WHC), and transpiration.
