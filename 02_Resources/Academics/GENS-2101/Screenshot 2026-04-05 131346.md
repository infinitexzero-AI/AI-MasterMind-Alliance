# Toward ‘Integrated waste management’
- 3 R's: reduce, reuse, recycle
- A 4th R: recover
- Montreal and Calgary redirect less than one-third of waste from landfill sites
- Toronto redirects about 40%
- Halifax is slightly better at approximately 55%
- Markham, Ontario impresses at 70% redirection
  - Strategy includes regulations regarding packaging, and blue box/green bin pick-ups 2Xs more often than regular waste pick-up.
