# Biofuel energy production, 2024
- This map illustrates total biofuel energy production for 2024, measured in terawatt-hours (TWh) per year.
- Biofuel production includes both bioethanol and biodiesel.
- Countries are color-coded based on their production levels, with a legend ranging from 0 TWh to 200 TWh+.
- Major producers (100 TWh+ to 200 TWh+) appear to be the United States, Brazil, China, India, and Indonesia.
- Many other countries show low production or are marked as 'No data'.
- The data source is the Energy Institute - Statistical Review of World Energy (2025) and OurWorldInData.org.
