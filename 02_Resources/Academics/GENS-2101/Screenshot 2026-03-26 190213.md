# Rainfall Patterns and Crop Growth Stages
- Three bar charts display daily rainfall (mm) from June to October across different years.
- Each chart highlights a "dry spell" during a critical plant growth stage:
    - **1995:** A dry spell is noted at germination (late June - early July).
    - **1996:** A dry spell occurs during grain filling (mid-September).
    - **Unlabeled Year:** A dry spell is indicated during flowering (early - mid-July).
- The charts visually represent how periods of low rainfall can coincide with key developmental phases of a crop, illustrating environmental impact on agricultural processes.
