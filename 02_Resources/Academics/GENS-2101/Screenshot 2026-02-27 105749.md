# Great Lakes - St. Lawrence Basin Map
- Detailed hydrological map showcasing the Great Lakes and St. Lawrence River system, including various sub-basins across the United States and Canada.
- Identifies major lakes (Superior, Michigan, Huron, Erie, Ontario), key rivers (St. Lawrence, Ottawa, Niagara), major cities (e.g., Toronto, Montreal, Chicago, Detroit), and critical water management infrastructure such as Compensating Works, International Control Works, and Moses-Saunders Dam.
- Features international and state/province boundaries, connecting channels, and the full Great Lakes-St. Lawrence Drainage Area, complete with a legend and scale.
