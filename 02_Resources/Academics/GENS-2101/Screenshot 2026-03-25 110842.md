# Global Agricultural Production in 2020: U.S. vs. Brazil

- **U.S. Agricultural Production (2020):**
    - Accounted for $134.7 billion, approximately 0.6% of national GDP.
    - World's largest corn producer (360.2 million tons), representing about one-third of global production.
    - Produced over half of the global almond output.
    - Produced over one-third of global blueberry production.
- **Brazil Agricultural Production (2020):**
    - Reached $85 billion in value, a 30% increase from 2019.
    - Agricultural land expanded to about one-third of Brazil's total area, a 22% increase over nine years.
    - Produced one-third of the world's soybeans (121.7 million tons).
    - Produced coffees (3.7 million tons).
    - Produced oranges (16.7 million tons).
