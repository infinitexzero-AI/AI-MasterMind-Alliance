# Doctrines of Water Allocation (Cont'd)

- **Limited Territorial Sovereignty/Integrity:**
  - A state has the right to the utilization of the waters of a shared river so long as its use does not compromise a co-riparian's ability to also use the water.
- **Community of interests:**
  - States' boundaries should be ignored and the drainage basin should be considered the economic and physical unit. Where an intervention is planned, it should be done in consultation with all basin members.
