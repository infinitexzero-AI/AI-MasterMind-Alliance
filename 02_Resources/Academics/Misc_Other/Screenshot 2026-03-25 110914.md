# Global Biofuel Production
- Bar chart illustrating the trend of global biofuel production from the year 2000 to 2023.
- The Y-axis represents production in thousand barrels equivalent per day, ranging from 0 to 1,000.
- The chart shows a consistent and significant increase in global biofuel production over the observed period.
- Production starts at a very low level in 2000 and steadily rises, reaching close to 1,000 thousand barrels equivalent per day by 2023.
