# Canada Revenue Agency Information Guide

## More information
- Provides contact details for income tax and benefit return inquiries: canada.ca/taxes, canada.ca/my-cra-account, and phone numbers (1-800-959-8281; 1-866-426-1527 for territories).

## If you move
- Instructions to update your address at canada.ca/cra-change-address.

## If you have new information and want to change your return:
- Options include online via canada.ca/change-tax-return or writing to your tax centre (find locations at canada.ca/cra-offices).

## If you want to register a formal dispute:
- Guidance to register a dispute within 90 days at canada.ca/cra-complaints-disputes.

## Help for persons with hearing, speech, or visual impairments
- Information on obtaining notices in alternative formats (braille, large print, electronic text, audio) at canada.ca/cra-multiple-formats.
- Teletypewriter contact number for tax information: 1-800-665-0354.

## You can pay:
- Lists various payment methods: online/phone through financial institutions, online at canada.ca/cra-my-payment or canada.ca/my-cra-account, or in person at financial institutions/Canada Post.
- General payment information: canada.ca/payments.
- Assistance for those unable to pay in full: call 1-888-863-8657 or visit canada.ca/cra-collections.

## My Account
- Explains how to use My Account to manage tax information online, including changing returns, checking RRSP info, and setting up direct deposit. Register at canada.ca/my-cra-account.

## Did the Canada Revenue Agency really contact you?
- Warns about scams impersonating the CRA via phone, email, text, or instant messages.
- Advises caution and provides resources to learn about legitimate CRA contacts at canada.ca/be-scam-smart.
