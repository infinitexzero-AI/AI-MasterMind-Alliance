# Wetland Offsetting
- The Mitigation Sequence: Must be applied to the fullest extent when considering wetland offsetting.
- Steps in the Mitigation Sequence:
    - 1. Avoid: Impacting wetlands to begin with
    - 2. Minimize: Impacts that cannot be avoided
    - 3. Mitigate: Impacts that cannot be reduced
    - 4. Offset: Any residual and unavoidable impacts
- Visual elements include a frog on a lily pad, waves, and logos for Ontario Nature and the University of Waterloo Faculty of Environment.
