# NB and NS gov comparison cont'd

## New Brunswick
- **Wetland Classification System:**
    - Provincially Significant Wetlands (PSW)
    - Other wetlands (functional assessment approach)
- **Protection Standard for Highest Category:**
    - PSWs: Avoidance prioritized; development highly restricted
- **Compensation / Offsetting:**
    - Required where impacts cannot be avoided; emphasis on no net loss of function
- **Mapping & Screening Tools:**
    - WAWA Reference Map (screening tool only; field verification decisive)

## Nova Scotia
- **Wetland Classification System:**
    - Wetlands of Special Significance (WSS) (e.g., salt marshes, protected area wetlands, habitat for species at risk)
    - Other wetlands
- **Protection Standard for Highest Category:**
    - WSS: Alteration generally not permitted (with very limited exceptions)
- **Compensation / Offsetting:**
    - Compensation required for approved alterations; restoration or creation required to offset loss
- **Mapping & Screening Tools:**
    - Provincial wetland inventory and WSS identification tools
