# Net Capital Loss Carrybacks
- This page on TurboTax (Self-Employed 2025) allows users to apply net capital losses from the 2025 tax year to prior years.
- It states that any losses not applied to the three preceding years can be carried forward.
- The net capital loss currently available for carryback is $0.00.
- Input fields are provided to specify amounts for losses to be applied to 2022, 2023, and 2024 (all showing 0.00).
- The page includes 'Back' and 'Continue' navigation buttons, and a sidebar with various tax return sections.
