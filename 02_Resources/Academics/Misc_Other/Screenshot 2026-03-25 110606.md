# Global Cotton Production
- A choropleth map illustrates worldwide cotton production, measured in 480 lb. bales.
- A five-tier color legend categorizes production levels:
    - 0-8,000 bales
    - 8,000-55,000 bales
    - 55,000-350,000 bales
    - 350,000-1,000,000 bales
    - Over 1,000,000 bales (represented by the darkest shade).
- Major cotton-producing countries (darkest shade) include the USA, Brazil, Turkey, China, India, and Australia.
- Significant production also occurs in regions like Mexico, Egypt, Sudan, Central Asia, and Pakistan.
