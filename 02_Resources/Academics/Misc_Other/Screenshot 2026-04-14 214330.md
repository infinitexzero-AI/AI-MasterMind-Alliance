# Profit and Loss by Customer - East Coast Fresh Coals (January 1 - December 31, 2024)

This two-page financial report details the Profit and Loss statement for "East Coast Fresh Coals" for the entire year 2024. It presents a comprehensive breakdown of monthly and total financial performance.

## Key Sections:

### 1. Income
-   **Revenue Sources:** Includes Sales, Services, and Unapplied Cash Payment Income.
-   **Adjustments:** Accounts for Discounts given and Refunds-Allowances.
-   **Total Income:** Aggregates all revenue sources and adjustments.

### 2. Cost of Goods Sold (COS)
-   **Direct Costs:** Details costs related to Labour, Subcontractors, and Supplies/Materials for goods sold.
-   **Total COS:** Sums all direct costs.

### 3. Gross Profit
-   Calculated as Total Income minus Total Cost of Goods Sold.

### 4. Expenses
-   **Operating Expenses:** A comprehensive list including administrative (e.g., Accounting Fees, Bank charges, Legal and professional fees), marketing (e.g., Advertising, Promotional), operational (e.g., Rent, Repair and maintenance), and other general expenses (e.g., Bad debts, Interest expense, Meals and entertainment, Office expenses).
-   **Travel and Utilities:** Specific expense categories for Travel, Meals, and Utilities are also detailed.
-   **Total for Expenses:** Sums all listed expenses.

### 5. Other Income
-   A line item for additional income sources.

### 6. Other Expenses
-   Includes miscellaneous expenses such as Credit Card Fees, Medical, and Reconciliation Discrepancies.

### 7. Profit
-   The final profitability figure, presented for each month and as an annual total, derived after accounting for all income and expenses.

This report provides a granular view of the company's financial health, illustrating revenue generation, cost management, and overall profitability across the specified period.
