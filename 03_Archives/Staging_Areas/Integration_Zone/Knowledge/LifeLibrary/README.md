# Joel's Life Library

**Created**: 2025-12-05  
**Version**: 1.0

## Overview

This is your personal Life Library - a comprehensive, organized knowledge architecture spanning all domains of your life.

## Domain Structure

### 🎓 Academic Wing

University courses, research, papers, notes, and academic development

**Subdomains**: courses, research, assignments, notes, textbooks, projects

### 💼 Professional Wing

Career development, business ventures, professional networking, and skills

**Subdomains**: business_ventures, career_development, networking, skills_training, certifications, portfolio

### 🏠 Personal Wing

Life management, health, finance, relationships, and personal growth

**Subdomains**: health_fitness, finance, relationships, hobbies, travel, life_admin

### 🤖 AI & Technical Wing

AI projects, AILCC framework, code, technical learning, and development

**Subdomains**: ailcc_framework, ai_projects, code_repositories, technical_learning, experiments, documentation

### 🎨 Creative Wing

Writing, design, media projects, and creative ideas

**Subdomains**: writing, design, media_production, ideas_brainstorming, art, music


## How to Use

1. **New Files**: Drop them in `_Inbox/` - they'll be auto-sorted
2. **Active Work**: Use `_Active/` folders in each domain for current projects
3. **Archive**: Older files are automatically organized by date
4. **Search**: Use the Life Library dashboard for full-text search

## Storage Tiers

- **Hot** (frequent access): Local + iCloud sync
- **Warm** (occasional): External drive  
- **Archive** (rare): Google Drive
